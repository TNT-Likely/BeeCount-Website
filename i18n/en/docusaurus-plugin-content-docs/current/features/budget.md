# Budget Management

Set budgets to control spending and develop healthy financial habits.

## Total Budget

Set a monthly total expense budget and track spending progress in real-time.

1. Go to "Discover" â†’ "Budget Management"
2. Tap "Set Total Budget"
3. Enter your monthly budget amount

## Category Budgets

Set budgets for specific categories, such as:

- Food: $500/month
- Entertainment: $200/month
- Shopping: $300/month

## Budget Alerts

- Get notified when spending reaches 80% of budget
- Warning displayed when over budget

## Budget Cycle

Budgets are calculated by calendar month and automatically reset on the 1st of each month.
